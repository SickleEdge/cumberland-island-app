First Aid Information

  • • Always carry a first aid kit
  • • Know basic first aid procedures
  • • Stay hydrated and protect from sun exposure
  • • Be aware of heat exhaustion symptoms
  • • Know how to treat insect bites and stings
  • • Keep any wounds clean and covered

Weather Emergencies

  • • Check weather forecasts before your visit
  • • Be prepared for sudden weather changes
  • • Seek shelter during thunderstorms
  • • Avoid beach areas during high tide
  • • Be aware of hurricane season (June-November)

Wildlife Encounters

  • • Maintain safe distance from all wildlife
  • • Do not feed or approach animals
  • • Be aware of alligator presence near water
  • • Watch for snakes on trails
  • • Report injured or distressed wildlife

Emergency Preparedness Tips

  • Always carry identification and emergency contact information
  • Know your location on the island
  • Stay with your group
  • Carry a whistle for signaling
  • Have a plan for meeting points
  • Keep your phone charged (though service may be limited)
  • Know the ferry schedule and last departure time

Need More Information?

For the most up-to-date emergency information and procedures, please contact the park directly or visit the official National Park Service website.
